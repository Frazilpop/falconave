About

Falcon Ave. are a musical band founded by Fraser Mackenzie. He is accompanied by a revolving selection of musicians and friends. He performs live with both a full band and also solo acoustic.

Based around Bedford and Milton Keynes, Falcon Ave. are know for their blend of folk and alternative rock music, as well as Fraser Mackenzie's narrative-heavy style of songwriting.

Members/ contributors to Falcon Ave. are as follows:

Fraser Mackenzie – songwriting, vocals, guitar
Szymon Targosh – guitar, vocals
Aran Lamond – keyboard/ piano
Jack Melville – bass guitar, vocals
Ed Norman – drums/ bongo
Harvey Butterfield – drums/ bass guitar
Chynna Rowe – vocals/ tambourine


Soundtracks

Falcon Ave. have also created the soundtracks for the following video games:

Lion Quest (2016)
Lion Quest Versus Expanded (2017)
M1: A Death in the Desert (2018)
Sloth Tales (2020)
Lion Quest Infinity (2021)
